## 💡 KEYWORD TARGETING TIPS

### Rule 1: Geo-Tag Everything
Always include location:
- ✅ "Best salon in Vijayawada"
- ❌ "Best salon"

### Rule 2: Match Search Intent
If someone searches "salon near me", they want:
- Location-based results
- Quick decision
- Map/directions
- Reviews & ratings

### Rule 3: Long-Tail Keywords Win
- Long-tail: "keratin treatment cost in vijayawada" (easier to rank)
- vs Short-tail: "keratin treatment" (harder to rank)
- Focus on 3-4 word phrases

### Rule 4: Address Competition
Who ranks #1-3 for your keywords?
- If big sites (Justdial, Google My Business): focus on reviews + local SEO
- If smaller sites: you can outrank with better content
